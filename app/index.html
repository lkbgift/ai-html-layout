About Us

Acquinity Interactive is a product marketing and development company, with many platforms of expertise: niche online communities, their accompanying email titles, telemarketing, and consumer services in the fields of sweepstakes, health, couponing and political polling. At Acquinity, we leverage our unique demographic targeting and distribution channels to ensure that each consumer has a genuine interest in our advertisers’ offerings.

We own and produce numerous Web portals; chief amongst them bSaving.com, Sweepstakes.com, HealthProfessor.com and Political.com. Acquinity operates call centers across the U.S. and abroad.

Our advertiser base spans numerous verticals...daily deals, health & wellness, financial, travel, insurance, education, telecom and entertainment to name a few. Our audiences are extremely cost-conscious, gravitating to bargains, discounts, coupons and free samples.
